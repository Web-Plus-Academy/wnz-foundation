About Us

Empowering the Next Gen of Tech Leaders

WebNexZ Foundation exists to democratize web development education through community, mentorship, and a commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

To create a world where aspiring developers, regardless of their financial background or location, have access to quality education, mentorship, and a supportive community that helps them thrive in the tech industry.

Our Mission

To build a self-sustaining ecosystem where fellows learn, build, teach, and lead—creating a continuous cycle of knowledge sharing and community growth that produces industry-ready web developers.

Why We Exist

The tech industry needs more than just coders—it needs leaders, mentors, and community builders.

Access to Quality Education

Breaking barriers to make web development education accessible to everyone, regardless of background.

Discipline & Commitment

Building habits that transform passionate beginners into professional developers.

Community-Led Growth

Learning together, mentoring each other, and growing as a collective unit.

Saredufy Web Plus Academy Pvt Ltd

WebNexZ Foundation Fellowship is a community-driven learning initiative, supported by Saredufy Web Plus Academy Pvt Ltd for academic and mentorship excellence.

What Foundation Provides

  • Community-driven learning environment
  • Fellowship structure and governance
  • Peer learning and leadership opportunities

What Saredufy Provides

  • Comprehensive curriculum design
  • Professional mentors and instructors
  • Technical infrastructure and resources
Note: There are no forced paid programs. The fellowship is free for selected candidates, and participation remains voluntary throughout.
