About Us

Empowering the Next Gen of Tech Leaders

WebNexZ Foundation exists to democratize web development education through community, mentorship, and a commitment to excellence.

Foundation Leadership

Guiding the community with clarity, responsibility, and long-term vision.

Rupa Sree .D

Community Director & Chairperson

Rupa Sree leads the WebNexZ Foundation as the Community Director & Chairperson, playing a key role in shaping the culture, structure, and long-term vision of the foundation.

She focuses on building a disciplined, inclusive, and mentorship-driven community where fellows grow not only as developers, but also as responsible leaders who give back to the ecosystem.

Our Vision

To create a world where aspiring developers, regardless of their financial background or location, have access to quality education, mentorship, and a supportive community that helps them thrive in the tech industry.

Our Mission

To build a self-sustaining ecosystem where fellows learn, build, teach, and lead—creating a continuous cycle of knowledge sharing and community growth that produces industry-ready web developers.

Why We Exist

The tech industry needs more than just coders—it needs disciplined professionals, mentors, and community builders who can learn, lead, and uplift others.

Access to Quality Education

We exist to break financial and social barriers by making high-quality web development education accessible to motivated learners, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Discipline & Commitment

Talent alone is not enough. We help learners build discipline, consistency, and professional habits that transform passionate beginners into dependable developers.

Community-Led Growth

We believe growth is strongest when driven by community. Fellows learn together, mentor each other, and grow as a collective—creating a sustainable cycle of learning and leadership.

Supported by #SWPA

Saredufy Web Plus Academy Pvt Ltd

The WebNexZ Foundation Fellowship is a community-driven learning initiative that operates independently with academic and mentorship support from Saredufy Web Plus Academy Pvt Ltd. This collaboration ensures strong educational quality while preserving the foundation’s non-commercial, learner-first mission.

What WebNexZ Foundation Provides

  • Community-first learning culture
  • Fellowship structure, governance, and values
  • Peer learning, mentorship cycles, and leadership roles

What Saredufy Web Plus Academy Provides

  • Industry-aligned curriculum framework
  • Experienced mentors and technical instructors
  • Learning resources, tools, and infrastructure
Important Note: This fellowship does not mandate any paid programs. Participation is completely voluntary, and selected fellows can complete the program without financial obligation at any stage.
